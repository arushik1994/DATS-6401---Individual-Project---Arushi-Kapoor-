In conclusion, it can be stated that US will continue to lead in terms of Total GDP, GDP Per Capita and Overall Spending. China's growth rate has been considerably higher in all types of spending. Therefore, we can conclude that China is the fastest growing nation in terms of total GDP and Overall Spending. However, it's GDP Per Capita tends to remain the lowest among all the 10 nations. We can also conclude that nations with a higher GDP Per Capita also tend to have a higher Education and Healthcare Spending Per Capita. Certain developed nations such as Australia, Germany and Canada tend to be consistent in their education and healthcare expenditure.
